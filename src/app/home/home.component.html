How to get started?

In order to begin you have to login to your account.
Start with adding your assets in Assest tab and see how the total value of them is updating according to the current exchange rates. Create alerts about specific value of exchange rates that interest you in Alerts tab. You can always change the main currency in your Profile settings to see howmoney do you actually have!
